Review: Basketball dog (2018)

4 out of 5 stars

From director Vicki Fleming comes the heartwarming tale of a boy named pete (Trent dugson) and his dog rover. You may think a boy and hisdog learning the true value of friendship sounds familia, but a big twist sets this flick apart: Rover plays basketball, and he's doggone good at it

This movie has everything you could ask for:

  • Basketball
  • A dog
  • Nail-biting suspense

While it may not have been necessary to include all 150 minutes of rover's championship game in real time, Basketball Dog will keep your interest for the enirety of its 4-hour runtime, and the end will have any dog lover in tears. if you love basketball or sports pets, this is the movie for you.
